# Image Prompts for Squished

Use these prompts in ChatGPT to generate new illustrations. Each prompt includes a style reference to match the existing art.

**Style reference to paste before each prompt (or set as a custom instruction):**
> Soft watercolour children's picture book illustration on a white background with soft vignette edges. Sandy beach setting with pale blue ocean, distant hills, and small tufts of sea grass. The character is a cute orange hermit crab with big expressive dark eyes and small orange claws. Hand-painted watercolour texture with warm, gentle colours. No outlines, no text.

---

## 1. harold-scuttling.png
**For the page:** "The next morning, Harold made a decision. He scuttled along the shoreline, looking. Searching."

**Prompt:**
Soft watercolour children's picture book illustration on a white background with soft vignette edges. A cute orange hermit crab with big determined dark eyes is scuttling across a long sandy beach, viewed from the side. He's in a brown spiral shell that looks slightly too tight on him. The beach stretches ahead of him with a few empty shells scattered in the distance. Pale blue ocean behind, distant hills, small tufts of sea grass. He looks purposeful - on a mission. Hand-painted watercolour texture with warm, gentle colours. No outlines, no text.
